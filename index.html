My Portfolio

Here's all the cool stuff I have built.

Anthems

Anthems was website that I built as a part of MATAHACK's first ever virtual hackathon. This project was deemed the best overall project granting, winning a first place selection. The theme of the hackathon was communication, and Anthems was website that displayed those features beautifully.

Anthems had many wonderful components. Perhaps, the best being the jukebox displayed on the homepage. There a user is allowed to control the flow of a set of randomly selected music. Allowing the user to get a taste of music they may have not experienced prior.

Another key component was a polaroid that had a group of new artists that were linked to each artist's spotify pages. Other pages included a product page, a blog page, and a page that displays our personal rankings for best music projects of the week.

Undeniable Project

This project was one I conceptualized during the BLM protests. The most beautiful part about software engineering is that our projects should represent the pulse of the people. I realized there was a need for mentorship and I hoped to provide it.

I prompted some of my nearest and dearest friends to provide a quote, a message, words of encouragement, and general advice for the youth of today. That message was then displayed in a popup similiar to this one. It is important for the youth to see young adults to look up to. Even more than that, it is important for young adults to share advice and experiences with today's youth.

The website was made to be a lot darker than a lot of my other pages. Reason being, I wanted to make certain words stand out. The aesthic was important to convey the message of site.
